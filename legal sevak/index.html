About

TLC can make it easier and more convenient to find and hire lawyers, and to get the legal services they need. TLC can provide clients with a wide range of lawyers to choose from, and they can provide information about lawyers' qualifications, and experience. TLC can also make it easier for clients to communicate. For legal service providers, TLC can provide a platform to reach a wider audience of potential clients and market their services. TLC can also help legal service providers to improve their efficiency and productivity. By meeting the needs of both clients and legal service providers, TLC for legal services can play a vital role in making legal services more accessible, affordable, and efficient.

Problems Observed

Indian legal system is a vast community which has many well qualified people who can serve people by providing their services. But these legal service providers were never have been exposed to any open online platform which can provide them more exposure in the market. Hence we observed a gap between clients and legal service providers that they are not connected online on a easy to access, secure and accountable common platform.

Proposed System

This proposed system will have the following features:-
- Improving accessibility
- Reducing costs
- Improving efficiency
- Educating clients
- Reducing corruption
Overall, the proposed project has the potential to make legal services more accessible, affordable, efficient, and transparent in India. This would benefit both clients and legal service providers.
